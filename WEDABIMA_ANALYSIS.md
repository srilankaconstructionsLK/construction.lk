# Competitor Analysis: wedabima.com

This document provides an analysis of `wedabima.com`, a major competitor/reference in the Sri Lankan construction directory space.

---

## 1. High-Level Strategy
`wedabima.com` uses an aggressive **SEO-first approach**. Every category, service, and location combination has its own dedicated landing page. This allows them to capture long-tail search traffic like *"Concrete slab work price in Gampaha"* or *"JCB for rent in Kandy"*.

---

## 2. Core Categories
The site is divided into several main sections, which go beyond a simple business directory:

*   **Rent Machines**: Focused on heavy equipment (JCBs, Excavators, Tippers, Mixers).
*   **Power Tools**: E-commerce/Directory for equipment sales (Drills, Grinders, Saws).
*   **Contractors**:
    *   **Special Contractors**: Large-scale firms (Steel buildings, MEP, Piling).
    *   **Sub Contractors**: Individual skilled labor (Masons, Plumbers, Carpenters).
*   **Building Materials**: Suppliers for raw materials (Cement, Bricks, Sand, Rubble).
*   **HR & Education**: Job vacancies, job seekers, and industry-related courses.

---

## 3. Data Insights (Example: Nandana Construction)
*   **Business Profile**: [Nandana Construction](https://wedabima.com/hardware/NTE3/Nandana-Construction)
*   **Services Listed**: Concrete Slab Work, Steel Building Construction, Warehouse Construction.
*   **Contact Info**: Phone numbers are highly visible (e.g., `0779594207`).
*   **SEO Keywords**: They list "Popular search by Location" at the bottom of every item, linking to Gampaha, Colombo, Kandy, etc.

---

## 4. Key Takeaways for Construction.lk
To compete with or exceed `wedabima.com`, we should consider:

1.  **Lead Generation Focus**: Wedabima acts as a middleman for leads. Our "Request for Quote" system could be more streamlined.
2.  **Location-Based SEO**: We need to generate dynamic pages for `[Service] in [District]` to capture search traffic.
3.  **Machine Rental Section**: This seems to be a high-demand area in Sri Lanka.
4.  **Verified Status**: While Wedabima is a directory, `construction.lk` can win on **CIDA Verification** and professional vetting, which Wedabima seems to lack in its basic listings.
5.  **User Experience**: Wedabima's UI is cluttered and SEO-heavy. We can win by providing a premium, clean, and mobile-first user experience (as per our design goals).
